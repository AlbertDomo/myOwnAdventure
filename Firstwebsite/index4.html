Road Blocks

Now although I was really enjoying my time at Art and Design, that didn't necessarily mean all was swell. I did come across many road blocks including anxiety and depression. I was feeling too intimidated by the beautiful pieces my classmates would present and would always think my art wouldn't ever be as good so it led me into this deep void. I ended up having not enough credits to graduate with the rest of my fellow classmates...
